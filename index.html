Mask Or Die



â €â €â €â €

Mask or Die is a game development project submitted to SacHacks, a Sacramento Hackathon where there is a time constraint of 48 hours to design and code a project. My team and I utilized C# and Unity to code the mechanics of the game, creating a first-person, mask-shooting game as a non-profit PPE for the Sacramento community in order to raise awareness of COVID-19 cases.

The team utilized Google Maps SDK to import the map of Sacramento, California, focusing on the environment around and with the capital building. As the player walks around in the game, the Google Maps SDK will build the streets of Sacramento around them, looking exactly as it does in the 3D view of Google Maps.

While undoubtedly a passion project, this taught me many practical lessons while developing it.
